# Fantasy Draft Pro — Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: July 17, 2026

You can request deletion of your Fantasy Draft Pro account and its associated data at any time.

## How to Request Deletion

Email justin6577@gmail.com from the address associated with your account, with the subject "Delete my account," and we will process your request.

## What Is Deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes your login credentials, connected league links, draft and pick history, watchlist, push notification tokens, and subscription entitlement records within 30 days.

## What May Be Retained

Records we are required to keep for legal, tax, or security reasons may be retained for the period required by law. Subscription transaction records held by Apple/Google (via RevenueCat) are governed by their own retention policies, not ours.

## Need Help?

Email justin6577@gmail.com with any questions about your data.
