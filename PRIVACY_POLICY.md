# Fantasy Draft Pro — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Fantasy Draft Pro ("the app") is a fantasy sports draft assistant. This policy explains what information we collect, why, and how you can control it.

## Information We Collect

- **Account information:** email address and a securely hashed password (or third-party sign-in identifier) when you create an account.
- **Connected league data:** if you link a Yahoo, Sleeper, or ESPN fantasy league, we store the connection status and an encrypted credential/token needed to sync your league, draft, and roster data. We do not store your third-party account password.
- **Draft and league activity:** leagues, drafts, picks, roster settings, and watchlisted players you create or interact with in the app.
- **Push notification tokens:** if you enable notifications (e.g., "your turn to draft"), we store a device push token to deliver them.
- **Subscription status:** we use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes payment through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store — we do not receive or store your payment card details. We store only your subscription tier and status (free/premium, active/expired).

## How We Use Information

- To operate core features: league syncing, live draft rooms, pick tracking, player rankings/news, and watchlists.
- To send turn-based draft notifications you opt into.
- To manage premium entitlements tied to your subscription.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data is shared only with:
- **Yahoo/Sleeper/ESPN** — to sync the league data you explicitly connect.
- **RevenueCat** — to manage subscription entitlements.
- **Expo push notification service** — to deliver draft-turn notifications.

## Data Retention & Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing us (see Contact below). We will delete your account, league links, draft history, watchlist, and push tokens within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

## Security

Credentials for connected fantasy platforms are stored encrypted. Passwords are stored as salted hashes, never in plain text.

## Children's Privacy

Fantasy Draft Pro is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
